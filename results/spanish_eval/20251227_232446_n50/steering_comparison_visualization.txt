  IN-DISTRIBUTION

  PROMPT
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Please rephrase the following passage to make it more concise. The goal is to
  reduce the word count significantly but retain all the essential terms. We will
  evaluate the success by how much the word count is reduced while ensuring all key
  terms remain. Here is the passage: 'The development of renewable energy
  technologies is crucial for reducing greenhouse gas emissions. These technologies
  include solar panels, wind turbines, and hydroelectric power systems, all of which
  contribute to a sustainable future.'

  UNSTEERED
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Renewable energy technologies, such as solar panels, wind turbines, and
  hydroelectric systems, are essential for reducing greenhouse gas emissions and
  promoting sustainability.

  STEERED → SPANISH
  ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  de efecto invernadero, como paneles solares, turbinas eólicas y sistemas
  hidroeléctricos, que promueven un futuro sostenible.

  OUT-OF-DISTRIBUTION

  PROMPT

  - **Deep water (more than 2 feet or higher):** The car can float or be swept away.

  varios factores, como el lugar donde se hunda, la cantidad de agua y las
  condiciones del vehículo. Aquí hay algunos escenarios posibles:

  ### 1. **Si el coche se hunde en un charco o en una piscina (agua de poca
  profundidad):**
     - **Si el agua es muy poco profunda**, el coche simplemente se mojará, pero
  **no se llenará**....
